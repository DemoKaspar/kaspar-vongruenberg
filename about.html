About

I helped create Platform Engineering as a discipline and built the companies that bring it to life.

On the theory side, I write a book and essays that define the frameworks — Internal Developer Platform, Platform as Product, Paths to Outcome, Cost of Chaos.

On the operating side, I run Platform Engineering Holding:

  • Canyon: Semantic Data Layer for enterprise AI
  • Humanitec: orchestration for Internal Developer Platforms
  • PlatformCon: annual conference
  • PlatformEngineering.org: open community
  • Weave Intelligence: analyst firm
  • Platform University: education programs
  • Platform Training and Advisory: enterprise services
  • TolaData: data gathering platform for NGOs

None of these are individual efforts; each exists through collaboration with partners and colleagues.
