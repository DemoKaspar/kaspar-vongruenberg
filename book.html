Book

I am writing a multi-part book on Platform Engineering as an operating model for knowledge work.

The book attempts to separate theory from vendor practice. It focuses on first principles: what a platform is, how it structures collaboration, and how it evolves inside large organizations.

Structure

It is structured in three parts:

  • Origins: why platforms must be treated as products
  • Model: a conceptual framework ("Paths to Outcome")
  • Practice: how to build and scale a platform inside an enterprise

Approach

The aim is not to promote a specific implementation, but to clarify the underlying logic that governs all of them.

Publication
