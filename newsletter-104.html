Newsletter Issue #104

Building Platforms with Kaspar (GCP, Port, Cursor, Claude Code, Humanitec, Datadog and friends)

Hey {NAME},

A couple of years ago I worked on the reference architecture with our friends at McKinsey's platform team. It resonated a ton with many of you. My guess is because it gave a concrete picture of how Internal Developer Platforms can actually look from an architectural lens.

Since then, I've kept getting the same question: "Can you show us real IDPs, end-to-end?" So I decided to do exactly that. I carved out 8 hours a week (big shout-out to my wife for approving this 😅) and started a new show: Building Platforms with Kaspar.

What's the Show About?

In each episode I'll take a setup I've seen in the wild. Real-world IDPs powering dozens or hundreds of developers, and rebuild it as a demo. Then I'll walk you through the golden paths on that setup.

The first episode is live now. We're building on GCP, using:

  • 👍 Port as the frontend
  • 👍 Humanitec as the backend
  • 👍 Terraform for IaC
  • 👍 Datadog for observability
  • …and a couple of other tools.

The Golden Paths We'll Cover

  • 🙌 Creating a service from a template — ready to code in minutes
  • 🙌 Adding a new resource to an existing workload
  • 🙌 Spinning up an ephemeral environment

👉 Watch the first episode here

I'll release a new episode every two weeks. If you've built an end-to-end architecture you think deserves to be showcased, I'd love to feature you on the show. We'd just hop on a call, you walk me through your setup, and I'll recreate it.

Kaspar
