What to expect

It combines conceptual work with observations from building platforms in practice. Topics include:

  • Platform Engineering as the foundation for enterprise AI
  • Agentic development and the changing role of software engineers
  • The rise of business users as platform consumers
  • Organizational design for knowledge work
  • Field notes from enterprise roll-outs

Editorial Policy

I do not accept sponsors, paid placements, or "pay-to-play" contributions.

Some editions may reference work related to the organizations I run. When this is the case, it is stated explicitly and aligned with my Bias & Disclosure statement.
